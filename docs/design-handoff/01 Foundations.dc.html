The Vision

The dealership operating system, not another car marketplace.

One connected product: a premium buying experience for shoppers, and the CRM, inventory and analytics engine that runs the dealership behind it. Every customer action lands in the dealer's dashboard instantly — nothing is disconnected.

Site Map & Information Architecture

Two experiences, one data layer. Everything under Dealer OS is gated behind staff login; everything under Consumer is public.

Personas

Two people, two halves of the same product.

Maya Torres
Car shopper, 34 · researching a family SUV
Goal: Find a trustworthy vehicle, understand true monthly cost, book a test drive without calling anyone.
Frustration: Dealer sites feel like 2010; financing numbers are hidden until she calls.
Moment of delight: Instant financing estimate + one-tap test drive booking, confirmed in seconds.
Derek Owusu
Sales manager, 41 · runs a 6-person floor team
Goal: Know which leads are hot, keep his team accountable, close more without extra admin.
Frustration: Leads from the website, calls, and walk-ins live in three different tools that never sync.
Moment of delight: A test drive booking appears in his pipeline the instant it's submitted — already assigned.

The Connected Journey

Maya's actions on the left instantly become Derek's work on the right — the same event, two views.
