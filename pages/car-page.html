Buyer's Guide — History / Overview

Mercedes-Benz redesigned its mid-size E-Class into a 10th generation in 2017, spanning sedan, wagon, coupe, and convertible body styles. Recent model years focused on trim and standard-feature updates rather than a full redesign — expect more tech as standard across the range, including a heated steering wheel and wireless phone charging.

What's new — the entry-level model was renamed E 350, more standard tech was added across trims, and option packages were revised.
